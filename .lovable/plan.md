
# Lumina — AI Mentor for Career Guidance

A calm, trustworthy presence that guides students through their career journey with warmth and wisdom.

---

## 1. The Welcome Experience

**First Impression (Landing Page)**
- A single, centered message that speaks directly to the student's heart — something like *"You don't have to have it all figured out. Let's explore together."*
- Soft amber glow with cream tones, no navigation clutter
- A gentle invitation to begin — not a button, but almost a whispered prompt
- Ambient, breathing animation in the background (subtle light movement, like candlelight)

**Gentle Onboarding**
- Simple sign-up with email — framed as "So I can remember our conversations"
- No forms bombardment — just name and email, nothing more
- Warm welcome message from the mentor after signup

---

## 2. The Conversation Space

**The Study Room**
- Full-screen, distraction-free conversation area
- No sidebar, no header crowding the space
- Soft, rounded text areas that feel like writing in a journal
- The mentor's responses appear letter-by-letter, like someone carefully typing to you
- Student's messages feel like handwritten notes, not chat bubbles

**Conversation Feel**
- No timestamps cluttering the view
- Generous spacing between exchanges
- Smooth scrolling that feels like turning pages
- Optional gentle background ambient sounds (toggleable)

---

## 3. Backend & Data

**User Accounts & Persistence**
- Simple email/password authentication via Supabase
- Conversation history saved so the mentor "remembers" past discussions
- User profiles to personalize the experience over time

**AI Integration**
- Streaming responses for that gentle, thoughtful typing feel
- Context-aware conversations using chat history
- Warm, mentor-like system prompt that maintains the calm, guiding tone

---

## 4. Visual & Interaction Details

**Color Palette**
- Primary: Soft amber (#F5E6D3) with warm cream (#FFFBF5)
- Accents: Deep warm brown for text (#5C4033)
- Subtle gold highlights for interactive elements

**Typography**
- Elegant serif for the mentor's voice
- Clean sans-serif for interface elements
- Generous line-height for breathing room

**Animations**
- Buttery-smooth 300ms+ transitions
- Subtle fade-ins, never abrupt
- Gentle hover states that feel alive, not reactive

---

## Summary

This is not an app. It's a mentor who waits patiently in a quiet room, ready to listen and guide. Every pixel should feel intentional, every interaction should feel human, and every student should leave feeling hopeful.
